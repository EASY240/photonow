    Pro Tips for Better AI Product Photos

    Optimize Your Input Image

    The quality of your input image significantly impacts the final result. Follow these guidelines:

    • Use even, diffused lighting to minimize harsh shadows
    • Capture the product against a simple, contrasting background
    • Ensure the entire product is in frame and in focus
    • Clean the product to remove dust, fingerprints, or smudges

    Craft Effective Text Prompts

    The text prompts you provide guide the AI in creating your scene. Here's how to make them effective:

    • Be specific about materials: "marble countertop" instead of just "countertop"
    • Describe lighting: "soft natural light from the left with subtle shadows"
    • Mention atmosphere: "minimalist kitchen setting with morning light"
    • Include brand-relevant details: "luxury hotel bathroom with gold accents"
